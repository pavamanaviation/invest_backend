1. Period
The lease shall be for a period of 66 months, commencing from {{ preview.from_date }} to {{ preview.to_date }}.
2. Rental
The Lessee shall pay:
  • ₹25,000/- monthly (inclusive of GST/TDS), within 10 days after each month for 66 months; OR
  • ₹22,44,000/- (₹34,000/month for 66 months) at the end of 64 months, payable at the end of 66 months, subject to TDS deductions.

3. Residual Value
The residual value at the end of the lease is ₹1,56,000/- per drone. The Lessee may purchase the drone in "as-is" condition in the 67th month.
4. Taxes
Taxes shall be deducted as applicable.
5. Termination by Lessor
The lease may be terminated by the Lessor with 45 days’ written notice if:
  1. The Lessee fails to pay rent for 3 consecutive months.
  2. The Lessee is declared insolvent.
  3. Or, for any reason, with 3 months’ advance written notice.
On termination, the Lessee shall return the drone to the Lessor at Survey No. 121, Sreeramnagar, Moinabad Mandal, Rangareddy – 500075, and settle any pending dues.

6. Rights of the Lessor are non-transferable without Lessee’s written consent.

8. All operational risks lie with the Lessee during the lease period.
9. The Lessor may inspect the drone with 3 days' prior written notice.
10. Notices must be in writing and sent to registered addresses.
11. This agreement is binding and can only be amended in writing.
12. Disputes unresolved within 15 days will be settled by arbitration under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996. Jurisdiction: Rangareddy District Courts.
